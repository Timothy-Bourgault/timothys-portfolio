Self

My love for design has brought me to a place in my life where I am driven by form and function, all while maintaining a close connection to the catharsis which brings about true beauty and understanding within the human spirit. I am a junior designer and front end developer living in Portland, Oregon. I'm addicted to learning and teaching others what I've learned. I have over 10 years of experience with interactive product design, A/B test design, front end development, user testing, creative experiments, successes, and failures.
